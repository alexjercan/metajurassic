Frequently Asked Questions

What is Metajurassic?

Metajurassic is a daily guessing game where you try to identify a mystery dinosaur species. Each guess reveals taxonomic relationships on a phylogenetic tree, helping you narrow down the target.

How do I play?

Type the name of a dinosaur species into the input box and press Enter. The tree will update to show how your guess relates to the mystery species. Use the clades revealed in the tree to guide your next guess. You have 25 attempts to find the target.

What does the tree show?

The tree displays the phylogenetic (evolutionary) relationships between your guesses and the mystery species. Clades are shared ancestor groups. The closer your guess is to the ? node on the tree, the closer you are to the answer.

What is the info panel on the right?

Clicking any node in the tree opens a panel with details about that species or clade. After each guess the panel automatically shows the best hint — the clade directly above the ? node.

Is there a new puzzle every day?

Yes! A new mystery species is selected each day. Come back tomorrow for a fresh challenge.
