Unique for having three different types of teeth: small incisors, sharp canine-like tusks, and flat grinding teeth for plants.During the harsh, dry seasons of the Early Jurassic, some paleontologists believe this tiny, tusked dinosaur might have actually burrowed underground and entered a state of hibernation to survive!
