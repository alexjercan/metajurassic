---
species: Pentaceratops
translation: Five-Horned Face
clade: chasmosaurinae
period: Late Cretaceous (76–73 Ma)
size: 6 meters
weight: 5,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/pentaceratops.jpg
---

Its name implies five horns, though two are actually protrusions of the cheek bones; it possessed one of the largest skulls of any land animal.
