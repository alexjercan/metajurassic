---
species: Saltriovenator
translation: Saltrio Hunter
clade: ceratosauria
period: Early Jurassic (199–197 Ma)
size: 7.5 meters
weight: 1,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/saltriovenator.jpg
icon: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/silhouette/species/saltriovenator-icon.svg

Discovered in Italy, it is the oldest known large-bodied ceratosaur, proving that meat-eating dinosaurs evolved into massive, multi-ton giants much earlier than scientists originally thought.
