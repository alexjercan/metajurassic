---
species: Sinraptor
translation: Chinese Raptor
clade: metriacanthosauridae
period: Late Jurassic (161–157 Ma)
size: 7.6 meters
weight: 1,300 kilograms
image: https://images.dinosaurpictures.org/Sinraptor/Sinraptor_7e70feca.jpg

Despite its name, it is not a "raptor" but an early allosauroid; it was a dominant predator of the Jurassic Asian floodplains.
