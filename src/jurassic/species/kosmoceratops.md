translation: Ornate Horned Face
clade: chasmosaurinae
period: Late Cretaceous (76 Ma)
size: 5 meters
weight: 2,500 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/kosmoceratops.jpg

Known as the most "ornate" dinosaur; its frill was decorated with 15 horns and spikes, including a unique row that curved downward.
