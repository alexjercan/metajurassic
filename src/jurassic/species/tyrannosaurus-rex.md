**Period:** Late Cretaceous (~68–66 million years ago)

Tyrannosaurus rex was one of the largest land predators to ever live. It had an
enormous skull, powerful jaws, and one of the strongest bite forces known in
terrestrial animals.

It belongs to the group Coelurosauria, a branch of theropods that includes
dinosaurs more closely related to birds. Earlier relatives of T. rex likely had
feathers.

With advanced senses of smell and vision, Tyrannosaurus rex was a highly
efficient predator at the very end of the age of dinosaurs.
