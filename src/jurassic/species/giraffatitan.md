---
species: Giraffatitan
translation: Giant Giraffe
clade: brachiosauridae
period: Late Jurassic (154–142 Ma)
size: 23 meters
weight: 40,000 kilograms
image: https://images.dinosaurpictures.org/Giraffatitan/Giraffatitan_d85f2f15.jpg

Often mistaken for Brachiosaurus, this African giant was significantly taller and is the focus of the world's tallest mounted skeleton.
