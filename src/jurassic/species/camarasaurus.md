species: Camarasaurus
translation: Chambered Lizard
clade: macronaria
period: Late Jurassic (155–145 Ma)
size: 23 meters
weight: 20,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/camarasaurus.jpg

The most common sauropod in Late Jurassic North America, its name refers to the massive hollow "chambers" (pleurocoels) inside its spine that kept its enormous body surprisingly lightweight.
