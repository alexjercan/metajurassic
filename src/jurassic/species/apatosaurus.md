species: Apatosaurus
translation: Deceptive Lizard
clade: apatosaurinae
period: Late Jurassic (152–145 Ma)
size: 21 meters
weight: 22,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/apatosaurus.jpg

A massive, robust sauropod; its name "Deceptive" comes from its tail bones, which resemble those of sea-dwelling mosasaurs.
