species: Daspletosaurus
translation: Frightful Lizard
clade: tyrannosaurinae
period: Late Cretaceous (77–74 Ma)
size: 9 meters
weight: 3,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/daspletosaurus.jpg

Sharing its habitat directly with Gorgosaurus, it likely avoided competition by acting as the heavy "bruiser" of the ecosystem, specializing in taking down heavily armored horned dinosaurs while its cousin chased faster prey.
