---
species: Fukuisaurus
translation: Fukui Lizard
clade: styracosterna
period: Early Cretaceous (125 Ma)
size: 4.5 meters
weight: 400 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/fukuisaurus.jpg

This Japanese dinosaur had a completely rigid skull, meaning unlike its later, more advanced duck-billed cousins, it couldn't chew its food with a complex side-to-side grinding motion.
