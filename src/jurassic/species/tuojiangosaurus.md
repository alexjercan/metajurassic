species: Tuojiangosaurus
translation: Tuo River Lizard
clade: stegosauridae
period: Late Jurassic (161–155 Ma)
size: 7 meters
weight: 2,800 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/tuojiangosaurus.jpg

The best-preserved stegosaur ever found in Asia; its skeleton was so complete that it gave scientists their very first perfect look at how Chinese stegosaurs differed from American ones.
