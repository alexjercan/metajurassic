---
species: Olorotitan
translation: Giant Swan
clade: lambeosaurini
period: Late Cretaceous (70–66 Ma)
size: 8 meters
weight: 3,100 kilograms
image: https://images.dinosaurpictures.org/Olorotitan/Olorotitan_55dad6f7.jpg
---

Named for its unusually long neck (containing 18 vertebrae); it was one of the last and most spectacular lambeosaurines in Asia.
