species: Maip
translation: Shadow of Death
clade: megaraptoridae
period: Late Cretaceous (70 Ma)
size: 9.5 meters
weight: 5,000 kilograms
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/species/maip.jpg

Named after an evil spirit from Tehuelche mythology, it is the largest megaraptoran known, possessing massive claws for disemboweling prey.
