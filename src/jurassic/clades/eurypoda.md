Diagnosed by a heavily constructed pelvis and the extreme shortening and broadening of the metatarsals (foot bones). This robust, widened foot architecture evolved strictly to support the immense, ever-increasing weight of their body armor; by completely sacrificing their ability to run away from danger, they were forced into a full-commitment defensive lifestyle that eventually led to weaponized tails.
