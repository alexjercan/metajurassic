---
clade: Plateosauria
parent: sauropodomorpha
---

Diagnosed by a lower jaw hinge that sits below the horizontal line of the teeth. Because this pivot point is offset, the upper and lower teeth meet simultaneously like a nutcracker, rather than slicing back-to-front like scissors. Combined with lengthened neck vertebrae, this clade took the next major anatomical step toward becoming dedicated, high-browsing herbivores.
