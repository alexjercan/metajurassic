clade: Iguanodontia
parent: cerapoda
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/iguanodontia.svg
---

Diagnosed by the complete loss of premaxillary (front) teeth, fully replacing them with a broad, keratinous beak. This allowed them to bulk-crop vegetation much faster than earlier ornithopods, fueling their growth into massive, highly successful medium-to-large herbivores
