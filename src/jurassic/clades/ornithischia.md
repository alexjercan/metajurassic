clade: Ornithischia
parent: dinosauria
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/ornithischia.svg
---

Diagnosed by a unique predentary bone at the tip of the lower jaw that supported a cropping beak. This primarily herbivorous lineage evolved a highly diverse array of physical defenses, including solid cranial domes, massive bone frills, dermal armor plates, and heavy tail clubs.
