Diagnosed by an incredibly complex, multi-tissue structure within the tooth battery itself, making the teeth dynamically self-sharpening across different wear angles. Because they were essentially the cows of the Cretaceous, their herds grew to staggering sizes, reshaping the landscape through sheer browsing pressure and migrating across vast continental stretches.
