Diagnosed by a massive increase in overall body size and limb robusticity, marking a strict evolutionary shift to an obligate quadrupedal posture. As their heads became drastically heavier with larger horns and frills, they were forced to drop down to all fours permanently, abandoning speed and relying entirely on heavy physical defense and herd behavior to survive.
