Diagnosed by deep, powerful skulls with massive, rod-like teeth. Often isolated on ancient European archipelagoes (like Hațeg Island), these robust herbivores dominated their island ecosystems, completely outcompeting other ornithopods in their restricted, insular environments and often experiencing island dwarfism.
