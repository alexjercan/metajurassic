clade: Stegosauria

Diagnosed by parasagittal rows (running parallel to the spine) of osteoderms that are heavily modified into tall plates and sharp spikes. While the lethal tail spikes (the "thagomizer") provided devastating active defense against predators like Allosaurus, the highly vascularized back plates likely doubled as massive thermal regulators or vivid, blood-flushed display structures.
