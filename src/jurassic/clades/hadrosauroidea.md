Diagnosed by the complete loss of the thumb spike (digit I) entirely, leaving a hoof-like, weight-bearing hand. By ditching the weaponized thumb, they fully committed to a quadrupedal browsing lifestyle, relying on massive herd numbers, rapid growth rates, and sheer reproductive volume to survive instead of fighting back.
