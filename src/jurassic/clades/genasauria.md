clade: Genasauria
parent: ornithischia
---

Diagnosed by medially inset teeth on both the upper jaw (maxilla) and lower jaw (dentary). This inset dentition allowed them to physically hold food in their mouths while chewing, a massive digestive upgrade that let them process tough plant matter far more efficiently than earlier herbivores and set the evolutionary stage for every major armored, horned, and duck-billed dinosaur that followed.
