Diagnosed by the secondary closure of the frill openings, creating a solid, fenestra-less bone shield, and extremely robust, elongated postorbital (brow) horns. By filling in the fragile holes in their frill, they transformed their heads back into true, battle-ready armor, allowing them to survive locked, head-to-head combat with the most devastating apex predator of their time: Tyrannosaurus rex.
