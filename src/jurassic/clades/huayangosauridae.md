---
clade: Huayangosauridae
parent: stegosauria
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/huayangosauridae.svg
---

Diagnosed by a shorter, deeper skull and the primitive retention of premaxillary teeth (teeth at the very front of the upper jaw). Retaining these front teeth allowed these early, smaller armored herbivores to actively nip and browse on a wider variety of tough vegetation before the lineage specialized into strictly beak-cropping feeders.
