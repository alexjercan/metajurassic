clade: Unenlagiina

Diagnosed by exceptionally long, slender snouts filled with small, unserrated teeth, and a unique shoulder joint that allowed the arms to fold completely flat against the body. This elongated, specialized snout strongly points to a piscivorous (fish-eating) diet, establishing them as the wading, heron-like predators of the southern continents.
