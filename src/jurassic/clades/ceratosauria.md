Diagnosed by prominent bony horns and brow ridges, the primitive retention of four fingers on each hand, and heavily fused ankle bones. This tightly bound lower leg structure provided exceptional shock absorption for explosive mobility, while their elaborate cranial ornamentation functioned as highly visible structures for species recognition and territorial display
