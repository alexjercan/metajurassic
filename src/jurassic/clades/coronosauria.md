Diagnosed by a heavily expanded posterior skull frill and the fusion of the first few neck vertebrae into a solid mass called a "syncervical." This fused bone provided an incredibly strong, rigid anchor point to support the massive weight of their ever-growing heads, allowing them to evolve gigantic display frills without snapping their own necks
