clade: Abelisauroidea
parent: ceratosauria
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/abelisauroidea.svg
---

Diagnosed by a noticeable shortening of the skull and extensive bony fusion between the shin and ankle bones. This heavily reinforced, rigid lower limb structure provided a highly stable foundation for explosive bursts of speed, allowing them to effectively ambush and sprint after prey.
