clade: Somphospondyli
parent: titanosauriformes
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/somphospondyli.svg
---

Diagnosed by highly pneumatized, spongy bone tissue within their presacral vertebrae. This extreme internal honeycombing drastically reduced the weight of their massive skeletons by up to 60% while maintaining structural strength, allowing them to support ever-increasing body sizes and heavily expanded digestive tracts for processing low-quality vegetation.
