clade: Brachiosauridae
parent: titanosauriformes
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/brachiosauridae.svg
---

Diagnosed by extremely long metacarpal bones and a crested skull with the nasal opening sitting at the very highest peak. Their towering, rigid framework made them the ultimate apex browsers, but this extreme vertical specialization made lowering their massive necks to drink ground water a difficult and highly vulnerable process.
