Diagnosed by a significantly enlarged, hatchet-shaped sternum and a noticeable increase in overall body size. This broadened chest cavity supported an expanded, highly efficient digestive system, allowing them to process vast amounts of low-quality forage and physically dominate their ecosystems through sheer bulk.
