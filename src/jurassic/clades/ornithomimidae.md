parent: ornithomimosauria

Diagnosed by completely toothless jaws and metacarpals (hand bones) of roughly equal length. These uniform hand bones formed a specialized, elongated hook-like structure, likely used to pull down branches for feeding in their highly successful herbivorous or omnivorous lifestyle.
