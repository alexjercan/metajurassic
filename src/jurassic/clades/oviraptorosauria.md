clade: Oviraptorosauria
parent: maniraptora
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/oviraptorosauria.svg
---

Diagnosed by shortened, deep skulls and highly specialized, toothless jaws often equipped with two bony prongs on the roof of the mouth. This reinforced, crushing beak structure allowed them to easily crack open hard food sources like seeds, nuts, or hard-shelled mollusks.
