---
clade: Dinosauria
---

Dinosauria is the major group of reptiles that dominated terrestrial ecosystems
for over 160 million years.

They are characterized by an upright posture, with legs positioned directly
beneath the body. Dinosaurs first appeared during the Late Triassic period and
diversified into a wide range of forms, including massive herbivores and agile
predators.

Modern birds are the only surviving members of Dinosauria.
