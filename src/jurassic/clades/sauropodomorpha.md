Diagnosed by leaf-shaped, coarsely serrated teeth and a massively enlarged, sickle-shaped thumb claw. While they began as small, bipedal omnivores, their specialized teeth allowed for a shift toward a strict plant-based diet. They initially used their powerful grasping hands to pull down branches, but this massive thumb claw was eventually reduced or lost as they evolved into the biggest land animals of all time.
