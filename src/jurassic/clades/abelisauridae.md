Diagnosed by incredibly deep, foreshortened skulls covered in heavy bony texturing (rugosity), alongside drastically reduced, stiff forelimbs that lacked functional wrist joints. To compensate for arms completely incapable of grasping, their thick necks and reinforced snouts evolved to deliver massive, blunt-force bites, driving their teeth deep into struggling prey through sheer impact.
