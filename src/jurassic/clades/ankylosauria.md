Diagnosed by the closure of the antorbital fenestra (a large opening in the skull in most other dinosaurs) and the fusion of the lumbar and sacral vertebrae into a solid rod called a synsacrum. This extreme skeletal fusion turned their entire torso into a rigid, unbendable platform, allowing them to support a literal shield of solid bone and easily survive being trampled or struck by massive theropods.
