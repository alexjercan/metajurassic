---
clade: Maniraptoriformes
parent: coelurosauria
image: https://alexjercan.github.io/metajurassic-images/clades/maniraptoriformes.svg
---

Diagnosed by a noticeably smaller skull relative to body size and highly enlarged brains. This increased neuro-processing power supported complex sensory adaptations like excellent vision and hearing, which were critical for tracking evasive prey or managing the varied diet of omnivory.
